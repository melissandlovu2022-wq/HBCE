Meet Our Founder

Melisa Ndlovu, the visionary founder and CEO of Honeybee Cleaning Enterprise (HBCE), launched the company in 2025 with a bold mission: to transform the cleaning industry while creating meaningful employment opportunities across South Africa. Her passion for excellence, community upliftment, and innovation drives every aspect of HBCE’s operations.

Under Melisa’s leadership, HBCE has grown into a trusted name in residential, commercial, and specialty cleaning services. Her commitment to empowering others and delivering top-tier service has made HBCE a beacon of hope and professionalism in the cleaning sector.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional cleaning services that uplift communities, promote hygiene, and create sustainable job opportunities for South Africans.

Our Vision

To become South Africa’s leading cleaning enterprise, known for innovation, integrity, and impact — where every sweep makes a difference.

Our Goals

  • 🌍 Reduce unemployment by training and hiring local talent.
  • 🧼 Set new standards in cleanliness, safety, and customer satisfaction.
  • 🏢 Expand our services across Gauteng and beyond.
  • 💡 Innovate with eco-friendly products and smart cleaning technologies.
  • 🤝 Build long-term relationships with clients through trust and excellence.

Core Values

  • ✨ Professionalism – We deliver with pride and precision.
  • 💛 Integrity – We do what’s right, even when no one’s watching.
  • 🚀 Innovation – We embrace new ideas and smarter solutions.
  • 🌱 Sustainability – We care for the planet while we clean.
  • 👥 Empowerment – We uplift our team and our communities.
